THE
CHALLENGE

The challenge of this hackathon is that each participating team(consisting of 1 to 4 members) must design, fabricate and program a robot within a 24 hour time period. The main goal of Robothon 5.0 is to offer a platform for robotic enthusiasts to showcase their talent and acquire new skills. Furthermore, we also believe that the intriguing backdrop of a hackathon will give birth to a few prompt and ingenious ideas.

LABS AND
FACILITIES

College of Engineering Chengannur is one of the 20 colleges in Kerala where Kerala Startup Mission has setup Fab Lab. Fab Lab is a space where creativity and imagination have no boundaries. Students are given access to the tools, training and community that they need to feel comfortable building just about anything. It aids to evolve technical and mechanical skills, develop creativity and find new ways to improvise and be innovative.

The Fab Lab is a production workshop and small-scale innovation hub equipped with digital fabrication machines and technologies for the production of objects, tools and electronics.

ROBOTHON 3.0
ON MEDIA

Robothon, the flagship 24 hour robotic hackathon of IEEE Student Branch, College of Engineering, Chengannur , the first ever of its kind in the nation was a huge success over its past 4 editions as it gave the robotic enthusiasts from around Kerala and beyond a platform to flaunt their skills. Robothon has seen a significant rise in the number of participants over the years and a good number of presented projects have gone on to achieve media coverage and what not! Take a flashback into the competition the past year and stay posted for a bigger and better - Robothon 5.0
