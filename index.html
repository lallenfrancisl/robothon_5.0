THE
CHALLENGE

The challenge of this hackathon is that each participating team(consisting of 1 to 4 members) must design, fabricate and program a robot within a 24 hour time period. The main goal of Robothon 5.0 is to offer a platform for robotic enthusiasts to showcase their talent and acquire new skills. Furthermore, we also believe that the intriguing backdrop of a hackathon will give birth to a few prompt and ingenious ideas.
